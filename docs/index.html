Welcome to FactorTHAT, an online tool to help you practice factoring.

  • Use the controls in the top right to enter your own number to factor or to generate a random number to factor.
  • Drag the prime factor tokens onto the number to factor it.
  • Use the less and more buttons to increase or decrease the prime factor tokens that are available to use.

Ready? Click on a button in the top right to get started!
